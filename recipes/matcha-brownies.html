Matcha Brownies

Description

The perfect gooey soft dessert bar for matcha lovers! Made with only 7 ingredients. Super simple, super delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp matcha
  • 1 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 350F
    2. Combine your dry ingredients
    3. Add melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract. Beat with hand mixer.
    4. Mix in white chocolate chips.
    5. Transfer batter to a lined cake pan.
    6. Flatten out the batter so that the surface is even.
    7. Bake at 350F for 35-40 minutes or until fully cooked. Remove from oven and let cool.
    8. Slice and enjoy!
