Brown Sugar Matcha Latte

Description

The perfect drink to make at home for when you're craving that bubble tea drink.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp matcha
  • 2 tbsp hot water
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • 1 cup milk of your choice (I like almond)
    •

    Steps

    1. In a small pot, heat up brown sugar with a little bit of water over low heat. Stir until the brown sugar has dissolved and the syrup has thickened. Take off the heat and set aside.
    2. In a small bowl, whisk together you matcha and hot water in a zigzag motion until matcha is well combined.
    3. Drizzle your brown sugar syrup over the sides of your glass and add your ice cubes inside.
    4. Pour in your milk, then top it off with your matcha.
